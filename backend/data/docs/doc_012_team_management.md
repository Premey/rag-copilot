# Managing Your Team

## Adding Team Members
1. Settings → Team → Invite Members.
2. Enter email addresses (comma-separated for bulk).
3. Assign a role: Admin, Agent, or Viewer.
4. Click Send Invites. Users receive an email with a join link (valid 7 days).

## Roles & Permissions
| Role | Can | Cannot |
|------|-----|--------|
| Admin | Full access, billing, settings | — |
| Agent | Manage tickets, view reports | Change billing/settings |
| Viewer | View tickets and reports | Create/edit/assign tickets |

## Removing a Team Member
Settings → Team → click the member → Remove. Their assigned tickets are automatically re-assigned to their team lead. You are not billed for removed users from the next billing cycle.

## Teams (Groups)
Create sub-teams (e.g., "Tier 1 Support", "Engineering") for routing tickets. Settings → Teams → New Team → Add members → Set round-robin or skill-based routing.
